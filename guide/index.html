The path

Eight lessons. Start at zero.

Follow them in order. Each one is short and plain. You'll leave each lesson with something you can try the same day.

  1. Read →01

    What AI is and where it breaks

    You'll learn what today's AI tools can handle, where they fail, and what to check before you trust one.

  2. Read →02

    Your first 15 minutes with an AI assistant

    You'll open a chat tool and get a useful result on a real small-business task, starting from nothing.

  3. Lesson 303

    The anatomy of a good prompt

    You'll see the five working parts of a prompt: role, task, context, format, and constraints. Then you'll know why some requests flop.

  4. Lesson 404

    Giving AI context about your business

    Show the tool your background, examples, and voice. Then its answers won't sound like they came from a blank brochure.

  5. Lesson 505

    Everyday uses that save real time

    You'll get low-risk workflows for common jobs, like drafting emails or summarizing documents after a long meeting.

  6. Lesson 606

    Checking AI's work

    Use one simple habit to catch confident mistakes and missing pieces, so you don't ship AI output blindly.

  7. Lesson 707

    Keeping your data and customers safe

    You'll learn what not to paste into public AI tools, and how to think about privacy as a business owner.

  8. Lesson 808

    Building your own prompt library

    Save and reuse the prompts that work. Your team won't have to start over each time.

Lessons 1 and 2 are live now. The rest publish here one by one. Bookmark this page if you're following along.
