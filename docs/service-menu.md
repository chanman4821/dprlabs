## 1. The one-line business

> **We build the AI agents and automations that run your business — then hand them to you to own.
> One number agreed up front. If it doesn't move, you don't pay.**

**The wedge (why this wins):** the market is full of AI agents you *rent* — 11x, Artisan,
Landbase, Salesforge. You pay monthly, the logic and data live in their dashboard, and the
moat compounds for *them*. DPR sells the opposite: **custom-built, owned** AI — the code,
weights, and eval suite handed to your team — priced against **one measured number**, with a
**no-move-no-fee** guarantee almost no competitor offers.

**Why it's proven (from the research):**
- Boutiques that **own the outcome** (measurable ROI) beat those that "just build models." ✓ DPR's one-number model.
- The most profitable structure is **productized entry → custom upsell → managed retainer.** ✓ Audit → Build → Retainer below.
- **Specialize, don't generalize** — "AI for anything" shops get beaten on price. DPR's specialization is *ownership + measured outcome* (and can narrow to a vertical later).
- What's dying: hourly billing, moonshot overpromising, rented-brain lock-in. DPR avoids all three.

## 3. How it's packaged & priced (the proven 3-step ladder)

### Step 1 — Opportunity Audit · **$2,500** (credited to the build)
Find the one workflow worth automating and the number to move. Deliverable: a written scorecard —
baseline, the target number, the recommended build, and a fixed quote. Low-risk entry.
_Market audits run $5k–$20k; DPR stays lean as the front door._

### Step 2 — Fixed-Price Build (pick a tier — all fixed scope, fixed price, **you own it**)
| Tier | What you get | Price |
|------|--------------|-------|
| **Starter** | One focused agent or automation (e.g. a support assistant, one ops workflow) | **$8,000–$12,000** |
| **Pro** | A multi-step agent with real integrations (e.g. an AI SDR wired to your CRM + inbox) | **$12,000–$25,000** |
| **Custom / Multi-agent** | Agent teams, custom pipelines, or compliance-heavy builds (+20–30% for regulated) | **$25,000–$60,000+** |

Add another workflow to any build: **+$2,500**.
_Market single-workflow builds run $5k–$15k; moderate $15k–$50k; DPR sits in the credible middle for senior (ex-Microsoft/PayPal) builders._

### Step 3 — Managed Retainer (optional) · keep it running, build the next
| Tier | Covers | Price |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Basic** | Monitoring, upkeep, light updates (1–2 automations) | **$2,000/mo** |
| **Growth** | Optimization + new workflows + priority support | **$3,500–$6,000/mo** |
| **Premium** | Multi-agent fleets, unlimited consult, tighter SLA | **$8,000+/mo** |

Cancel any time. _Market retainers run $2k–$15k/mo._

### The guarantee (the differentiator — keep it front and center)
**Miss the agreed number → no fee, and you keep the code.** Hit it → you own everything:
code, weights, prompts, evals, docs, deploy path.
