### H1

| | Copy |
|---|---|
| **CURRENT** | Production AI your business *actually* owns. |
| **PROPOSED** | AI built into your workflow. One number. Owned by you. |
| **Why** | "Actually" is defensive — it implies surprise. The proposed version states three concrete facts instead: built-in (not bolted on), one number (the method), owned (the guarantee). Each word can be backed with a page on the site. |

_Alternative if founder prefers a single declarative:_
> **"One workflow. One number. The AI is yours."**
> (9 words; the three-part structure mirrors the three hero proof bullets)
