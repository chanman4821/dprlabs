Why most AI projects stall

Most AI projects die as a demo.

Not because the tech can't work — because nobody agreed what "working" meant. A vendor sells a deck, runs a flashy proof of concept, and hands you something that never reaches production. Six months later you're still paying, still dependent, no closer to owning it. We built DPR AI to be the opposite: small scope, one agreed number, fast, and yours to keep.

30-second self-check

Is your workflow actually AI-ready?

Most "AI didn't work" stories are really "nobody agreed what working meant" stories. Answer four questions about your own workflow — you'll know in thirty seconds whether you have an AI problem or a scoping problem.
