About

A small, senior team allergic to theater.

We build production AI that your business owns — measured against one number, handed over on day done. No demos that die on a messy Tuesday, no rented black boxes.

Start a build → How it works

What we believe

Measured, not promised

One number, agreed in writing before code. If it doesn't move, you don't pay.

Production, not demos

Built into your real tools and repos from day one — not a slide that impresses in a meeting.

Owned, not rented

Code, weights, prompts, and evals go to your team. You can fire us and keep running.

What we won't do

Staff augmentation

We ship owned systems, not warm bodies by the hour.

6-month "transformations"

One workflow, weeks not quarters. Prove it, then scale.

No-baseline work

If we can't measure the before, we won't claim an after.

No-metric clients

No agreed number, no build. That's the whole model.

"Make us AI-first" mandates

We automate a real task, not a buzzword.

No-owner builds

Someone on your side has to own it after we leave.
