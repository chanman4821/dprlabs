# DPR AI — Go-To-Market (GTM) Plan

*Internal working plan. Not published to the site. All numbers are planning **estimates**
for a 2–3 person bootstrapped team, not guarantees. No clients/logos/testimonials/stats are
claimed. Capital-side activity is information, not investment advice.*

---

## 0. Bottom line

One funnel, in order: **free tools + honest content → paid build clients → a portfolio of
measured founders → investor subscribers.** Win the **build side first**; the capital side
is phase-2 demand the build side *earns*. Rushing the investor side before real
measured-founder proof exists breaks both honesty and compliance — that's the #1 risk.

## 1. Beachhead ICP (win this one segment first)

**US, founder-led B2B SaaS / vertical-software startups, pre-seed→seed, 3–25 people, with
paying customers and a product but NO dedicated ML/AI engineer.**

The person: the technical or product co-founder under board/customer pressure to "add AI,"
who shipped a rushed GPT-wrapper feature that embarrasses them and can't tell if it
actually works. Their pain: they need **one** AI feature that's **owned** (in their repo,
their keys), **measured** (a single honest quality/accuracy number they can show a customer
or investor), and **not slop**.

Why this wedge:
- **Fastest to revenue** — they have budget authority, move in days not quarters, and the
  $500–$2,500 Audit is an easy first yes.
- **Best flywheel proof** — they're exactly the "founders DPR has built for" that phase-2
  accredited investors want to see. Every build creates a fundable, measured company.

Explicitly **not** first: local-services SMBs (underpay, churn) or enterprise (sales cycles
kill a 2–3 person team).

## 2. Positioning & messaging

**Category we own:** *Owned, measured AI* — the anti-slop, proof-over-theater AI studio.
Positioned against agency "AI theater" and rented black-box SaaS.

**Core wedge message:** "We build AI you own and can prove — one honest number, no lock-in,
no slop."

**Differentiators (only real/shipped claims):**
1. **You own it** — ships in your repo, your keys, your infra. No black box, no rented seat.
2. **One honest number** — every build ends with a single measured quality metric you can
   show customers and investors, not a demo.
3. **Senior-only team** — the people who scope it build it. No offshore hand-off.
4. **Anti-slop by design** — evaluated against real data before it ships; we tell you when
   AI is the wrong answer.
5. **Free, working proof first** — the /free Inbox-to-Action agent and BYO-key tools are
   real software you can run today, not lead-bait.

**One-liners:**
- Build ICP: *"For seed-stage founders under pressure to add AI: we ship one AI feature you
  own and can prove works — in weeks, in your codebase."*
- Investor (accredited only): *"A free weekly AI-scored teardown of what we're seeing —
  information, not advice — for accredited investors who want signal over hype."*

## 3. Channels & plays (organic only — no paid ads)

### Build side (phase 1)
| Channel | The repeatable play |
|---|---|
| **Free tools as lead magnets** | Ship each tool open-source/BYO-key with a soft footer: "Built by DPR AI — want this owned and measured for your product? Book a free teardown." Post each release to the communities below. |
| **Content / SEO** | One deep, honest article/week tied to something DPR actually built or evaluated, targeting long-tail founder queries ("how to measure LLM feature accuracy," "own vs rent AI," "is my AI feature slop"). The auto-updating News page is the recurring-visit hook. |
| **Communities** | Be genuinely useful (not promotional) in Indie Hackers, relevant subreddits, seed-stage Slack/Discord, YC forums, local meetups. Answer 3–5 "how do I add/measure AI" questions/week; link a free tool only when relevant. |
| **Partnerships** | Fractional CTOs, seed dev shops that don't do AI, accelerators/incubators. Offer a free AI Audit for their portfolio → become their default "AI person" → referrals (no fees exchanged). |
| **Direct outbound** | 5–10 hand-written notes/week to founders who just shipped a visibly rough AI feature, referencing their actual product, offering a free 20-min teardown. No pitch deck. |
| **Referrals** | Every completed Audit/Build ends with an explicit ask for one intro to another founder with the same problem. |

### Capital side (phase 2 — compliance-bounded, opens only after portfolio proof)
| Channel | The repeatable play |
|---|---|
| **Weekly "What We're Seeing" memo** | Free, AI-scored teardown, information-not-advice, accredited-only sign-up. Same day each week. This is the *entire* top of funnel for capital. |
| **Warm network + syndicates / angel groups** | 1–2 warm intros/week of the memo as a reading resource. Never pitch a raise, never take a fee — signal only. |
| **LinkedIn / X** | Founder posts 2–3 genuine AI-market analyses/week; profile links to the accredited-only memo. No fundraising co-marketing with advisory. |

## 4. The two funnels

**Build side:**
Awareness (tool release / community answer / SEO article / outbound note) → **Interest**
(runs the /free agent or reads a KB article → *books a free AI teardown*) → **Trust** (the
free teardown delivers one real, honest finding → *accepts a paid Audit $500–2,500*) →
**Paid** (Audit → *signs a Build sprint $3k–15k*) → **Expansion** (Build → *signs a retainer
$2k–10k+* and gives a referral intro).

**Capital side:**
Awareness (LinkedIn analysis / warm intro) → **Interest** (*subscribes to the free memo*,
accredited-only) → **Trust** (12+ consecutive weeks of honest, non-advice teardowns) →
**Paid** (*upgrades to Signal $3k / Syndicate $7.5k / Inner Circle $12k per yr*) →
**Expansion** (renewal + tier upgrade). Never cross-sold into a raise.

## 5. Sales motion (founder-led, low-touch, productized)

Every step is a **pre-templated artifact** so a 2–3 person team never writes bespoke decks.

1. Free tool/content → **free 20-min teardown call** (founder delivers one specific, honest
   observation live).
2. → **Productized Audit** (fixed scope, fixed price $500–2,500 — no custom proposal).
3. Audit deliverable ends with **one recommended Build + its one honest target number.**
4. → **Build sprint SOW** (fixed scope/price).
5. On delivery → standing **retainer** offer ("keep the number honest over time") + referral
   ask.

Capital: the free memo runs ~12 weeks so the paid tier becomes an obvious upgrade; the
upsell is a single honest, information-not-advice, accredited-only email.

## 6. Launch sequence

### 90-day 0→1 (build-side traction only)
- **Days 1–30:** Polish /free Inbox-to-Action as the flagship proof. Publish 4 honest
  founder-focused articles + wire the News page. Start 5–10 personalized outbound teardown
  offers/week + daily community participation. **Goal: first paid Audits.**
- **Days 31–60:** Convert Audits → first Build sprints. Ship the AI Starter Kit as a second
  lead magnet. Sign first 2–3 accelerator / fractional-CTO referral partnerships. Turn every
  delivered Audit into a documented (client-approved, honest) measured result.
- **Days 61–90:** Convert first Builds → retainers. Systematize the productized ladder
  (fixed templates). **Only now begin drafting (not publishing) the capital memo**, using
  real build-side founders as future proof.

### 12-month arc
- **Q1:** build-side lead engine + first revenue.
- **Q2:** repeatable Audit→Build→Retainer motion, referral flywheel, a real portfolio of
  measured founders.
- **Q3:** launch the free weekly memo to the accredited network (capital side opens *now*,
  earned by portfolio proof); build subscriber base.
- **Q4:** convert 12+-week memo readers into paid subscriptions; let the
  build → founders → capital flywheel compound.

## 7. Numbers & KPIs (planning estimates)

**Per-stage targets (organic, first 90 days, build-side):**
- Awareness: ~500–1,500 unique visitors/mo to site + /free by day 90 (slow organic ramp).
- Interest: 30–60 free-tool activations/mo; 8–15 free teardown calls booked/mo.
- Trust → Paid: **3–6 paid Audits/mo** by day 90.
- Paid → Build: 30–50% of Audits convert → **~1–3 Builds/mo.**
- Expansion: 40–60% of Builds → a retainer.

**Rough CAC / effort per channel (organic = time, not dollars):**
- Free tools: ~$0 cash; ~3–5 build-days per tool + ~2 hrs/wk upkeep.
- Content/SEO: ~$0; ~4–6 hrs/article, 1/wk; payback slow (3–6+ months to rank).
- Communities: ~$0; ~3–5 hrs/wk.
- Direct outbound: ~$0; ~3–4 hrs/wk for 5–10 touches; ~5–10% reply → ~1–2 teardowns/wk.
- Partnerships/referrals: ~$0; ~2–3 hrs/wk; highest-quality, lowest-effort once seeded.
- Capital memo (phase 2): ~$0 cash; ~4–6 hrs/wk; CAC = consistency over ~12 weeks per sub.

**KPIs per stage:** Awareness = visitors, tool activations · Interest = teardown calls
booked, activation→call rate · Trust = call→Audit % · Paid = Audits closed, Audit→Build % ·
Expansion = Build→retainer %, MRR, referral intros/delivery. Capital = memo subscribers,
weekly open rate, 12-week retention, subscriber→paid %.

## 8. Highest-leverage first move (start Monday)

**Turn the live /free Inbox-to-Action agent into the lead engine:** add one honest CTA —
*"Want this owned and measured for your product? Book a free 20-minute AI teardown"* — and
personally send it to **10 named seed-stage founders** who've shipped a visibly rough AI
feature. This activates a real, shipped asset as top-of-funnel and books the first teardown
calls that seed the entire build-side flywheel.

## 9. What will be slow or hard (honest)

- Organic-only = near-zero paid reach; awareness compounds slowly (SEO payback 3–6+ months).
- Early pipeline depends almost entirely on founder-led outbound + referrals, which **don't
  scale past the team's hours.**
- Accredited-investor trust is slower still and is deliberately gated behind ~12 weeks of
  consistent memo output + a real measured-founder portfolio.

**Guardrails:** targets are estimates, not guarantees; no invented clients/logos/stats;
capital side is information not advice, accredited-only; DPR never promises a raise, never
takes a % of a raise or a per-intro fee, and never co-markets fundraising with advisory.
