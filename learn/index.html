Prompt Builder

Fill the parts of a good prompt — a copy-ready prompt builds live.

Your prompt — copy into a real tool

This tool only assembles your own words into a prompt. It never generates or fakes an AI answer — paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini to run it.

  • ✓Task — the one thing you want done. Without it the AI has nothing to do.
  • ✓Role — sets tone and focus in one line.
  • ✓Context — the details the AI can't know on its own.
  • ✓Constraints — length, do's and don'ts, boundaries.
  • ✓Format — the shape of the answer you want back.
  • ✓Tone — the voice it should use.
