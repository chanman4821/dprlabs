House rules

strip the chrome, hierarchy still reads

Do

  • One zone dominates each screen (60%+ of attention).
  • Hairline 1px detail; depth from value and light.
  • Restrained palette; spend the amber only on the number and one active state.
  • Honest, anonymised-but-real proof only.

Never

  • Glow orbs, neon mesh, particle fields, rainbow gradients.
  • Symmetric equal-card bento or cockpit rails.
  • Mono for prices, percentages, counters or headings.
  • Hierarchy from swapping font family.
