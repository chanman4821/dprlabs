# Contact — `contact.html`

*The single conversion endpoint. Goal: make the next step low-friction and honest — book a
call or email, get a straight answer in one business day, and know up front whether you're a
fit.*

*Grounded in `contact.html`. Deeper than the current page: expands the qualifier and the
"what happens next" so the visitor knows exactly what they're signing up for.*

---

## Section 1 — Page hero

- **Eyebrow:** Start a conversation
- **Headline:** Tell us one task your team dreads.
- **Subhead:** We'll reply within one business day with a straight answer.
- **Body:** Tell us whether AI can take that task off your team's plate, the one number we'd
  aim to move, and a fixed quote. No sales script, no obligation. If we don't think a pilot
  will help, we'll say that too — a straight no is faster and cheaper for both of us.

---

## Section 2 — Book a call, or just email us

- **Eyebrow:** Get started
- **Headline:** Book a call — or just email us.

**The form**
- *Name* — Your name
- *Work email* — you@company.com
- *What should we look at?* — The one repetitive task, workflow, or idea on your mind.
- **Primary CTA:** Send → routes to `hello@dprlabs.io`
- **Confirmation:** Thanks — we'll be in touch within one business day.
- **Direct line:** Prefer email? hello@dprlabs.io

**The qualifier (set expectations honestly)**
- **Good fit:** you have one repetitive, high-volume workflow — support, invoices, documents,
  data entry — that eats real hours every week, and a way to measure it.
- **Not yet:** you want a flashy demo with no metric, a project with no owner on your side, or
  AI for something a simple script would fix. We'll tell you so.

**Reassurance chips**
- 1 business-day reply
- Free opportunity read
- Your data stays yours · no sales script · no obligation

---

## Section 3 — What happens next (so it isn't a black box)

- **Eyebrow:** What happens next
- **Headline:** Three steps, one business day to the first answer.
- **Body (as a short list):**
  1. You send the one task on your mind. That's it — no long intake form.
  2. Within one business day, we reply with a straight read: whether AI can help, the number
     we'd aim to move, and a fixed quote.
  3. If it's a fit, we book a short scoping call and agree the number before any work starts.
- **Closing line:** No obligation at any step. The opportunity read is free; the pilot only
  starts once you've said yes to a number and a price.
