What DPR is

Two things, done properly.

Consulting

Build AI you can measure and keep

We build AI inside your own systems, agree on one number that shows whether it worked, and hand it over. Four kinds of work: automation, compliance and governance, low-code tools, and data integration. Paid monthly, not as a one-off project.

Investor network

Warm introductions, done honestly

We introduce the founders we work with to investors we know. Introductions only. We never negotiate terms, take a cut of a raise, or touch anyone's money. Investors pay a flat membership for a short list of vetted companies.

Why it's a real business

The two sides feed each other.

Good consulting work makes a founder worth backing, which lets us introduce them to investors we trust. When those bets pay off, investors trust our picks more, so better founders and better clients come to us. It runs on relationships, and those take years to earn. A consulting shop with no network has nothing to introduce. An intro service with no real work has no proof behind the names. Doing both well is the hard part.

What's real today

No fake clients. A low-risk way to prove it.

We're early, and we don't put made-up clients or logos on anything. Instead, we designed the offer so a first client takes almost no risk, and we point to the one real body of work we can show.

The pilot offer

Pick one number. We prove it moves. You own the code.

Two weeks. A fixed $8k–$15k, quoted up front. One workflow, one success number agreed in writing. Built inside your own systems. If the number doesn't move, you don't pay the fee — and you keep the code either way. The risk sits with us, which is why an early team is safe to hire.

Our flagship work

Design partner to NR3

DPR is a design partner to NR3, a separate live software platform, and runs it on real client work. It's the closest thing we have to a reference: proof we ship AI inside real systems, not slideware.

It's arithmetic, not a promise: a mix of monthly retainer clients plus a stack of memberships reaches roughly $200k/month. The full count and price assumptions — and the honest risks (a tiny base, long sales cycles in regulated fields) — are in the business & financial model.

Not a broker — and we mean it

The investor network is built to stay on the right side of the line.

Introductions and information only

We never take a percentage of a raise, a per-introduction success fee, or any pay tied to a financing. Investors are verified accredited, so introductions stay inside private-placement rules. Any startup we hold a stake in is a small, clearly-marked, unpaid part of what investors see, and every conflict is disclosed.

Not legal advice. DPR is not a broker-dealer and does not guarantee any raise. Full detail in Compliance & legal.

Why now

Companies want to own their AI, not rent a black box.

Spending on AI is up, but companies can't tell what's actually working, and they're wary of vendors who lock them in. New rules are landing in exactly the regulated fields we focus on — legal, healthcare, fintech, insurance. Our whole model is build-inside, prove-on-one-number, hand-over-so-you-own-it, which is precisely what a nervous enterprise buyer now wants.

Team

You work with the people who build, not an account manager. We won't put invented names or stock faces here — real, checkable profiles or nothing. Named founders and the roles this round funds are in the team page.
