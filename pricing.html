Pricing

Start small. Pay more only when it works.

Three steps, and you decide how far to go. A cheap audit to find the win. A fixed-price build to make it real. A monthly retainer only if you want us to keep going. You own everything we build, and you can stop any time.

Step 1 · Land

AI Audit

$500–$2,500
one-time, fixed fee

Find the one workflow worth automating. Before anyone builds anything.

  • We map your workflows and where AI actually pays off
  • A written scorecard: the first thing to fix, and the number it should move
  • A rough build cost, so there are no surprises
  • A straight go/no-go. Sometimes it's "don't build yet"

Best if you're not sure where to start. The fee credits toward a build if you go ahead.

Book an audit
Step 2 · Build

Build sprint

$3,000–$15,000
fixed price, fixed scope

One workflow, built into your systems and handed over. You own it.

  • We agree one honest number up front, then build to move it
  • It runs on your accounts, wired into the tools you already use
  • You get the code, the keys, and the docs. No lock-in
  • A before/after scorecard proves it worked

Price tracks scope: a clean single workflow near $3k; an integrated or compliance-heavy build up to $15k.

Step 3 · Keep

Monthly retainer

$2,000–$10,000+
per month, cancel any time

Keep it working. Build the next one. Only if you want us to stay.

  • We watch it and fix it as your data shifts
  • We keep it current as the AI models change underneath
  • We build your next workflow on the same terms
  • Cancel any time. You keep everything

Light monitoring starts near $2k. Active build across several systems runs $10k and up.

How the three fit together

Each step earns the next. The audit tells you whether a build is even worth it. The build proves it on a real number before you commit further. The retainer is optional, and it's month to month. You never buy the next step on faith.

Audit → Build sprint → Retainer (optional)

You own it

Code, keys, docs, all of it. Fire us on a Friday and your team can run it Monday. That's the point.

The guarantee

We agree the number before we build. If a build doesn't move it, you don't pay the final invoice, and you keep the code.

Fixed, not hourly

Every price here is a fixed fee for a defined piece of work. No open meter, no "strategic learnings" invoice.

We'll say no

If a script would do the job, or your data isn't ready, we'll tell you. An honest no is cheaper than a bad build.
