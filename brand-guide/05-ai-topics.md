## The posting engine

**Cadence — 5 posts a week:**
- 2 × scaling businesses (owned / measured)
- 1 × founder‑build
- 1 × funding intros
- 1 × contrarian AI reality check

Rotate stages so a week is roughly **3 awareness · 1 consideration · 1 decision**.

**Channels**

| Channel | Role |
|---------|------|
| LinkedIn | Primary — B2B decision‑makers and founders |
| X / Twitter | Contrarian takes and teardowns |
| Company blog / site | Canonical long‑form + proof pages |
| Email newsletter | Weekly digest: best post + one teardown |

**Repurposing — one idea, five surfaces.** Each week's teardown or long‑form is the anchor: it becomes a LinkedIn thread, an X hot‑take, a newsletter section, a checklist graphic, and a snippet on the matching site proof page. Written once, sharpened per channel — never copy‑pasted.

**Automation (no lock‑in).** DPR drafts each post with its own mesh from a plain‑English brief (topic, pillar, stage, hook, CTA) pulled straight from this doc, then a human editor approves before it ships. Scheduling runs through a swappable, open queue — no vendor lock‑in — and every post links back to an owned site asset the client keeps.
