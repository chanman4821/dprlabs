## How to use this

1. Pick the **logo** (see guide 1). My pick: the two‑tone **DP·R** wordmark + the blue **badge** as the app icon.
2. The **tokens** (guide 3) are already live in the site's CSS — nothing to change, just documented.
3. Use the **content guide** (4) to decide what to add to the site next.
4. Feed the **AI topics** (5) into a weekly posting schedule.

> **Plain‑English rule:** every word we publish should make sense to a smart 12‑year‑old. No hype, no jargon, no fake numbers.
